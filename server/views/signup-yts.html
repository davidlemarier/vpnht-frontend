Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a VPN?

Using a VPN is one of the best ways to hide what you’re doing online from your ISP and other users on your network, or make sure your traffic is secure on a public network. It’s also great to hide your IP address or bypass content filtering on a website or on your network.

The use of the BitTorrent protocol (used by Popcorn Time) for unauthorized sharing of copyrighted content (movies and tv shows) has led to a variety of novel legal issues. While the technology and related platforms are perfectly legal, law enforcement and prosecutorial agencies worldwide are developing new tactics and increasingly pursuing ways to address this avenue of infringement.

Source: Wikipedia and TorrentFreak

Is my connection encrypted?

Your connection will be completely encrypted to and from the VPN. Our default config provides the proper settings for complete encryption.

What do I get with my subscription?

Completely unfiltered access to our services. You can visit any website, any time. You can also use any kind of protocol, including torrenting and other services. You are also able to connect up to three devices to our network at any given time.

What measures do you take to protect my data?

By default we encrypt everything you do via our service using 128 bit AES end to end. Even our website secures all communication via A grade SSL. On top of that, we ensure that we do no logging of any kind, our logging is completely disabled in order to provide you extra security.

What kind of logging do you perform?

We don’t do any logging of any kind. We've taken steps to disable all logging on our services to ensure that you get completely anonymous usage of our services and to protect you from prying eyes.
